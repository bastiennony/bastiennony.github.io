I am a PhD student in Toulouse studying statistical modeling of plume transport in complex urban environments. I work under the supervision of Mélanie Rochoux, Didier Lucor and Thomas Jaravel. My PhD started in October 2019 in collaboration with CERFACS and LISN (CNRS/Université d'Orsay). I graduated from INSA Toulouse in applied mathematics and hold a MSc in Probability and Statistics (Université Toulouse III Paul Sabatier). I am interested in machine learning and uncertainty quantification applied to environmental problems.
